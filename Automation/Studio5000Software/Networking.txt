Allen Bradley PLC's are just microcontrollers connected to the internet or local subnet 

FactoryTalk and RSLinx are communication services/programs that enable you to communicate 
with the PLCs 

RSLinx Classic is legacy used for Old PCCC protocols like Remote IO, DH+, and DH-485. 
FactoryTalk Linx is the newest one and the next generation of RSLinx. Can only be used with 
modern CIP protocols like DeviceNet, ControlNet, and EtherNet.
